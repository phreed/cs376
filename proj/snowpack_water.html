melting snowpack 
function delta_mass_water = snow_melter(ut, m_ice, density_snowpack, delta_density, ...
      latent_freeze, vol_content, density_ice)
%SNOW_MELTER determine the change in water in the snowpack.
reduction = ut / latent_freeze ...
    * ((vol_content * density_ice) - density_snowpack) ...
    - m_ice * delta_density;
vol_density = vol_content * density_ice;
delta_mass_water = (vol_density * reduction) / ...
            (density_snowpack - vol_density)^2;
